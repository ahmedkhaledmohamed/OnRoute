Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

OnRoute ("we", "the app") helps you find places along your route, ranked by detour time. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

DataWhenPurpose
Search queries (origin, destination, category)Each searchFind places along your route

What We Don't Collect

  • No account or login required
  • No advertising identifiers or ad tracking
  • No background location tracking
  • No data sold to third parties
  • No social media data or contacts

Anonymous Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage data. Each device is assigned a random identifier (not linked to your identity). We track events like searches performed, categories used, and navigation handoffs to understand how people use OnRoute and improve the experience. No names, emails, or personal identifiers are attached to analytics data.

Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports. When the app crashes, diagnostic information (stack trace, device model, OS version) is sent automatically. This helps us find and fix bugs. Crash data does not include your search queries or location.

Email Collection

If you provide your email via the waitlist form or in-app prompt, we store it to send you product updates and launch notifications. You can unsubscribe at any time by emailing us. We do not share your email with third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps Platform (Routes API, Places API) — route calculation and place search. Privacy Policy
  • Apple MapKit (iOS) — map display and autocomplete. Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps SDK (Android) — map display. Privacy Policy
  • PostHog — anonymous analytics. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting. Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — hosting. Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Retention

  • Search queries: cached on our server for up to 1 hour, then deleted.
  • Saved routes and search history: stored on your device only. Not sent to our servers.
  • Analytics events: retained by PostHog per their data retention policy.
  • Crash reports: retained by Firebase for 90 days.
  • Email addresses: retained until you unsubscribe.

Children's Privacy

OnRoute is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of any data we hold about you by emailing us. Since analytics are anonymous and not linked to your identity, they cannot be individually deleted. Saved routes and search history are stored on your device — delete the app to remove them.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
