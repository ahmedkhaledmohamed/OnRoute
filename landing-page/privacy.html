Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

OnRoute ("we", "the app") is a free beta app that helps you find places along your driving, walking, or biking route. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

  • Search queries — origin, destination, and category (e.g., "coffee"). These are sent to our backend server to find places along your route.
  • Location data — only when you tap "Current Location." We do not track your location in the background.

What We Don't Collect

  • No account or login required
  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No background location tracking
  • No data sold to third parties

Third-Party Services

The app uses these services to function:

  • Google Maps Platform (Routes API, Places API) — to calculate routes and find places. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple MapKit (iOS only) — for map rendering and address autocomplete. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps SDK (Android only) — for map rendering. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Search queries are cached on our server for up to 1 hour to improve performance and reduce API costs. After that, they are deleted. We do not store search history long-term.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to delete or export. If you have questions, email us.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted on this page.
